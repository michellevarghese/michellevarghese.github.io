00 — About Me

Hi, I am Michelle

Large language models, agentic systems, multimodal AI, video understanding. If it is at the intersection of making AI actually work in the real world, I am interested.

I grew up in Bangalore saying yes to everything — hackathons, research, teaching web dev to anyone who would sit still. My undergrad research: an AI that watches you work out and judges your form. Spent months convincing people to do squats in front of a camera "for the advancement of science." Published it at IEEE. Won hackathons along the way. Figured out I loved building — and teaching people to build.

Post-undergrad: IDFC First Bank as a full-stack developer. Then NC State for my Masters, and a summer at LexisNexis working on systems handling 300M+ court dockets.

Now going deep on the hard stuff — LLM reliability, multimodal reasoning, vision + language + audio. Somebody has gotta solve them, might as well be me.
